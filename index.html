About me

I'm Shriyan Singh, a passionate and driven Data Science and Computer Science student at Stellenbosch University. Currently pursuing my Honours degree, I am excited about the vast potential of the Data Science industry and eager to contribute my skills to future projects.

Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong foundation in programming, data analysis, and machine learning. As a teaching assistant at Stellenbosch University, I have had the opportunity to guide and support students in their Computer Science and Machine Learning courses, further enhancing my technical skills and leadership abilities.

Beyond academics, I am a co-founder of Stellenbosch's first Hindu Student Society, where I served as the Students Affairs Manager. This experience has honed my communication, organization, and interpersonal skills, allowing me to effectively collaborate with diverse groups of people.

I am constantly seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and take on new challenges. My experiences at Stellenbosch University have fueled my passion for innovation and problem-solving using cutting-edge technologies. I am particularly interested in leveraging large datasets to uncover meaningful patterns and insights that can drive impactful solutions.
